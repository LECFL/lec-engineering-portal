Platform map

From legacy SIS to modern cloud apps.

LEC runs a constellation of internal Next.js, Firebase, GCP, Google Workspace, Stripe, SQL Server, MongoDB, and AI tooling. This portal turns that ecosystem into a navigable product map.

01

Student information

The legacy SQL Server SIS remains the source of truth for students, families, enrollment, attendance, staff, and payments.

02

Google Workspace

GAM frontend and backend automate users, Chromebooks, Classroom, groups, policies, calendars, and Drive workflows.

03

Communications

LEConnect dashboards, attendance notifications, SendGrid, SMS, WhatsApp, push, short URLs, and webhook logging support family communication.

04

AI operations

Golem, Bina, and LEC DB Knowledge provide AI-assisted engineering, support, memory, skills, and safe SIS context.
